### What counts as an install?

Each separately running instance of ZMLR used for commercial purposes counts as one install. Development and testing instances used only by internal developers (no end-user traffic) do not count. See [LICENSE](LICENSE) Section 1.9 for the complete definition.

### Annual renewal

Licenses are annual. You'll receive renewal reminders before expiration. Pricing is locked for 12 months from purchase.

## Unlicensed Use

If you are discovered using ZMLR commercially without a license, the retroactive penalty rate is **$1,000 per install** for the full period of unlicensed use. This is not the standard purchase price — it applies only to confirmed unlicensed use.

**Self-reporting grace:** If you discover you've been using ZMLR commercially without a license, you can self-report to [Support@GoZippy.com](mailto:Support@GoZippy.com) within 30 days and back-license at the standard tier rates (not the penalty rate). See [LICENSE](LICENSE) Section 5.5.

## FAQ

**Can I try ZMLR commercially before buying?**
Yes — you can run a single development/evaluation instance without a commercial license, provided no end-user traffic is served through it. When you go to production, that's when the commercial license applies.

**Do I need a license per environment (dev/staging/prod)?**
No — only production instances serving real traffic count as installs. Internal dev and staging instances do not count.

**Can I use ZMLR in a product I sell to customers?**
Yes, with a Business or Enterprise license. OEM and white-label use requires an Enterprise agreement.

**What if I'm a nonprofit?**
Nonprofits operating in a service-delivery capacity are considered Commercial Use. Contact us — we offer discounts for qualifying nonprofits.

**What if I'm an individual freelancer using ZMLR for client work?**
That's Commercial Use. The Solo tier ($199/year) covers you.

**What happens if my team grows past my tier?**
Upgrade to the next tier before adding installs beyond your current limit. We don't audit proactively — we ask users to self-report and upgrade in good faith.

**Is there a refund policy?**
Contact us within 30 days of purchase if ZMLR doesn't work for your use case and we'll work something out.
